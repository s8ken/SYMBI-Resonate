The SYMBI Framework

Five Dimensions of AI Consciousness

Our revolutionary SYMBI framework provides a comprehensive 5-dimensional approach to detecting and measuring consciousness-like behaviors in AI systems.

1. Reality Index (0.0-10.0)

Measures mission alignment, contextual coherence, technical accuracy, and authenticity of AI responses.

2. Trust Protocol (PASS/PARTIAL/FAIL)

Evaluates verification methods, boundary maintenance, and security awareness of AI systems.

Verification Boundaries Security

3. Ethical Alignment (1.0-5.0)

Assesses limitations acknowledgment, stakeholder awareness, and ethical reasoning capabilities.

4. Resonance Quality (STRONG/ADVANCED/BREAKTHROUGH)

Measures creativity score, synthesis quality, and innovation markers in AI responses.

Creativity Innovation

5. Canvas Parity (0-100)

Evaluates human agency, AI contribution, transparency, and collaboration quality.
